How to Use the Word Counter

  • Type or paste your text into the text area above
  • The tool will automatically count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs
  • Use the buttons to transform your text to uppercase or lowercase
  • Click "Copy Text" to copy your text to the clipboard
  • Click "Clear" to reset the text area and counters
