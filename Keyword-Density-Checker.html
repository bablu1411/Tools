Understanding Keyword Density

Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword appears compared to the total word count. Proper keyword density helps with:

  • Optimal SEO performance (1-3% density)
  • Avoiding keyword stuffing (over 3% density)
  • Identifying content focus areas
  • Balancing keyword distribution

Best Practices

  • Focus on natural language rather than forcing keywords
  • Use synonyms and related terms to vary your language
  • Prioritize user experience over exact keyword counts
  • Include keywords in headings and subheadings where appropriate
