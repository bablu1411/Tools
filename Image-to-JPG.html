Why Convert to JPG?

JPG (or JPEG) is one of the most widely used image formats because:

  • It offers good compression with adjustable quality
  • It's supported universally by all browsers and devices
  • It's ideal for photographs and complex images
  • It helps reduce file size for faster web loading
  • It maintains decent image quality even at smaller sizes

Quality Recommendations

  • 90-100 - Highest quality (large file size)
  • 80-89 - Excellent quality (recommended for most uses)
  • 70-79 - Good quality (smaller files with minor quality loss)
  • 60-69 - Moderate quality (noticeable compression artifacts)
  • Below 60 - Low quality (significant artifacts, very small files)
